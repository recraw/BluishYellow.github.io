Privacy Policy

Introduction

Thank you for using Random Guys. This Privacy Policy is intended to inform you of our policies regarding user privacy. We value your privacy and want you to understand that Random Guys does not collect, use, or share any personal information about you. By using the app, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

No Personal Data Collected

Random Guys is designed to be safe for children. We do not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information from any user, including children aged 3 and above

That means we never ask for or store:

  • Your name or nickname
  • Email address or contact information
  • Phone number or any device identifiers
  • Precise location or GPS data
  • Any other personal information or data about you

All data related to your use of Random Guys (such as game progress or settings) remains local on your device. We do not transmit or send any of your information off the device. Because no personal data is collected, we also do not sell, rent, or share your information with anyone.

COPPA & Children’s Privacy

Because Random Guys collects no personal information, we are not subject to the U.S. Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). No child under age 13 (or any age) needs to provide personal data to use our app. If you believe we have inadvertently collected personal information from a child under age 13, please have a parent or guardian contact us immediately at bluishyellowsoftware@gmail.com, and we will delete it promptly.

No Third-Party Analytics or Advertising

Random Guys does not include any third-party analytics tools, advertising networks, or trackers. We have not integrated services such as Google Analytics, AdMob, Facebook SDK, or similar. This ensures:

  • No outside parties monitor your gameplay.
  • You can enjoy the game entirely offline.

Platform Notices (Google Play)

If you downloaded Random Guys from the Google Play Store, please note:

  • Google may collect device or usage information as part of its platform services (e.g., crash reports, basic stats).
  • Random Guys does not receive or access any data Google collects.

For details on Google’s data practices, you can review Google’s Privacy Policy.

Parental Controls and Guidance

  • We recommend that parents or guardians supervise young children’s use of mobile apps.
  • Ensure that app permissions are set appropriately on your device (though Random Guys requests no permissions).

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make significant changes, we will:

  • Post the updated policy here.
  • Note the new “Last Updated” date at the top of this page.

Contact Us

If you or your child have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

Email: bluishyellowsoftware@gmail.com

We’re happy to help with any privacy-related questions or requests.

Enjoy playing Random Guys, and thank you for trusting us with your privacy!
