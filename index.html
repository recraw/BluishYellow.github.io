Privacy Policy

Introduction

Thank you for using Random Guys. This Privacy Policy is intended to inform you of our policies regarding user privacy. We value your privacy and want you to understand that Random Guys does not collect, use, or share any personal information about you. By using the app, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

No Personal Data Collected

Random Guys does not collect or store any personally identifiable information from users. All features of the game can be used without providing personal data.

This means we do not collect:

  • Your name or nickname
  • Email address or contact information
  • Phone number or any device identifiers
  • Precise location or GPS data
  • Any other personal information or data about you

All data related to your use of Random Guys (such as game progress or settings) remains local on your device. We do not transmit or send any of your information off the device. Because no personal data is collected, we also do not sell, rent, or share your information with anyone.

No Third-Party Services or Advertising

The Random Guys app does not use any third-party analytics tools or advertising networks. We have no integrated services (such as Google Analytics, AdMob, or other trackers) in the app. This means no outside party is collecting data through our app, and your gameplay is not monitored or analyzed by third-party services. You can enjoy the game offline without any data being sent to external services.

Google Play Store Notice

If you downloaded Random Guys through the Google Play Store, please be aware that Google may collect certain information as part of its platform and services. For example, Google Play (or your device’s operating system) might collect data such as general usage statistics or device information when you install or use the app. Random Guys itself does not collect this information, but it’s important to note that such data collection by Google is subject to Google’s own privacy policies. You can review Google’s Privacy Policy for more details: https://policies.google.com/privacy. We do not receive or have access to any personal data that Google may collect.

Children’s Privacy

Random Guys is intended for general audiences and does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. Since the app does not gather any data, it is safe for users of all ages from a privacy standpoint. We do not knowingly allow children under 13 to provide any personal information to us. If you are a parent or guardian and believe that your child has somehow provided personal information while using this app, please contact us at bluishyellowsoftware@gmail.com. We will take prompt steps to delete any such information, although in principle our app should not collect any data from users.

Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or suggestions about this Privacy Policy or the privacy practices of Random Guys, feel free to contact us. You can reach the developer at bluishyellowsoftware@gmail.com. We will be happy to answer your questions or address any issues regarding user privacy.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update or make changes to this Privacy Policy from time to time. If any significant changes are made, we will post the updated policy here. We encourage you to review this Policy periodically for any updates. Your continued use of Random Guys after any changes to this Privacy Policy will signify your acceptance of those changes.
